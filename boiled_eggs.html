1. First, place the eggs in a pot and fill it with enough water to cover the surface of the eggs.
  2. Next, turn on the gas or electric stove to heat the water in the pot until it reaches a rolling, bubbling boil.
  3. After that, reduce the heat to low and let the eggs cook in the boiling water for about 10-12 minutes for large-sized eggs.
  4. Finally, pour out the hot water and switch to pouring cold water over the boiled eggs until they are completely cool.
