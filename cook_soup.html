1. Add 750ml of water to a pot and bring it to a boil, then add the fish and parboil for a few minutes.
  2. Add 1 packet of Knorr Canh Chua (Sour Soup) seasoning to the pot, stirring well until the seasoning is completely dissolved.
  3. Finally, pour out the hot water and switch to pouring cold water over the boiled eggs until they are completely cool.
